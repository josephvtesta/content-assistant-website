Privacy Policy

For ContentAssistant.app and Content Assistant

Effective Date: 05/31/2023

This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect the information obtained from users of ContentAssistant.app and Content Assistant. By using our website and chrome extension, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect:

a. Personal Information: We may collect personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, or other contact details when you voluntarily provide them to us through ContentAssistant.app or Content Assistant.

b. Non-Personal Information: We may collect non-personal information such as your IP address, browser type, device information, and usage data through the use of Google Analytics. This information helps us analyze trends, administer the website and chrome extension, and improve user experience.

c. Chat Logs: When using Content Assistant, the conversations you have with the AI may be logged for improving the quality of the service. However, we will not store any personal information obtained during these interactions.

2. Use of Information:

a. Personal Information: We may use personal information you provide to respond to your inquiries, fulfill your requests, send administrative information, and communicate with you regarding our services, promotions, and updates.

b. Non-Personal Information: We may use non-personal information collected through Google Analytics to understand how users interact with ContentAssistant.app and Content Assistant, improve their functionality, and enhance our overall service.

c. Chat Logs: The chat logs collected from Content Assistant will be used solely for the purpose of improving the performance and accuracy of the AI model. We will not use these logs to identify individual users or associate them with any personally identifiable information.

3. Disclosure of Information:

a. Service Providers: We may share your personal information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating ContentAssistant.app and Content Assistant and providing our services. These service providers are obligated to maintain the confidentiality and security of your information.

b. Legal Requirements: We may disclose your personal information if required by law, regulation, or legal process, or in response to a valid subpoena or government request.

4. Data Security:

We take reasonable measures to protect the information we collect from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, please be aware that no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Third-Party Links:

ContentAssistant.app and Content Assistant may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these third-party sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any such sites you visit

6. Children's Privacy:

ContentAssistant.app and Content Assistant are not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information.

7. Changes to this Policy:

We reserve the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be effective upon posting the revised policy on ContentAssistant.app or Content Assistant. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we collect, use, and protect your information.

8. Contact Us:

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at marketing@contentassistant.app.

By using ContentAssistant.app and Content Assistant and providing your information, you consent to the collection, use, and disclosure of information as described in this Privacy Policy.
